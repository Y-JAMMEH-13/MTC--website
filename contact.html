Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join MTC?

Simply fill out the contact form above expressing your interest, or visit us during our meeting hours. We welcome all students passionate about mathematics and technology!

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! We welcome members of all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced learners. We have resources and mentorship for everyone.

When do you meet?

We typically meet Monday through Friday from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM after school hours. Special events and workshops may have different schedules.

Is there a membership fee?

MTC membership is free for all students! We believe in making mathematics and technology education accessible to everyone.
